The Doon School

Visit

The Doon School (informally Doon School or Doon) is a selective all-boys boarding school in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India, which was established in 1935.The Doon School is one of India's finest schools, with a strong intellectual heartbeat. The Doon School is a full boarding school for boys only.

The Asian School

Visit

The Asian School is among the best schools in Dehradun which provide co-educational Boarding school. The Asian School offers co-educational education from Nursery to XII and is affiliated to the CBSE Board.

Welham Girls' School

Visit

Welham Girls' School is a private boarding school for girls located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India.The school was established in 1957. Welham was named after the little Welsh village from where its founder, H.S. Oliphant hailed.

Ecole Globale International Girls' School

Visit

Ecole Globale International Girls School is a fully-residential girls' school situated in the educational capital of India, Dehradun. Established in April 2012, Ecole Globale is run under the aegis of the Asian Educational Charitable Trust.

Woodstock School

Visit

Woodstock School is an international coeducational residential school located in Landour, a small hill station contiguous with the town of Mussoorie, Uttarakhand, India, in the foothills of the Himalayas.
