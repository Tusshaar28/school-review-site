About Us

Schools4U is a platform made for users to find the right choice of school for themselves or their kids. Currently it allows user to rate the schools in Dehradun out of 5, and also allows them to add their own school.More features have been planned to roll out in future, including reviewing schools.

This website has been made as a mini project by Tushar Sharma of BTECH CSE Sec G in his 3rd Semester.It uses HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and XAMPP for its frontend and backend.
